What to Report

You can report observations of law enforcement misconduct, such as:

  • Excessive or unnecessary use of force
  • Unlawful stops, searches, or detentions
  • Harassment or intimidation by officers
  • Racial profiling or discriminatory treatment
  • Failure to identify or provide badge numbers
  • Destruction or confiscation of recording devices
  • Planting evidence or falsifying reports
  • Theft or property damage during searches

Document safely: If possible, note badge numbers, patrol car numbers, time, location, and any witnesses. Your safety comes first.
