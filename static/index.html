What to Report

You can report observations of concerning activity in your community, such as:

  • Groups engaging in suspicious behavior
  • Drug dealing or illegal transactions
  • Vandalism or property damage
  • Intimidation or threatening behavior
  • Weapons being displayed or used
  • Vehicles involved in suspicious activity

Emergency? If you are witnessing a crime in progress or someone is in immediate danger, please call 911 immediately.

Privacy Policy
